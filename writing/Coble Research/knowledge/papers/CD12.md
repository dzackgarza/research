# Rational surfaces with a large group of automorphisms

- **Used here for:** Cremona-special and unnodal Coble surfaces, the Halphen blowdown lemma, and the modular-curve description of Enriques and Coble cusps.

- **Project relevance:** provides geometric structure theorems for rational Coble surfaces and their automorphism groups.
