# Satake compactification and extension of holomorphic mappings

- **Used here for:** extending the lattice-induced embeddings on period spaces to morphisms of Baily-Borel compactifications.
- **Project relevance:** supplies the compactification-theoretic justification for the maps between $F_{\mathrm{Co}}$, $F_{\mathrm{En}}$, and $F_{(2,2,0)}$.
