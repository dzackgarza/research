> [!remark] Baily-Borel Boundary of F_{2d}
> Let $F_{2d}$ be the moduli space of degree $2d$ polarized [[K3 surface|K3 surfaces]]. Its Baily-Borel compactification $\overline{F_{2d}}^{\operatorname{BB}}$ has boundary strata corresponding bijectively to $\Gamma_{2d}$-orbits of primitive isotropic sublattices of $T_{2d}$ of ranks 1 and 2:
> - **0-cusps** correspond to primitive isotropic lines.
> - **1-cusps** correspond to primitive isotropic planes.
> 
> The incidence relations are set by lattice inclusions: a 0-cusp is contained in the closure of all 1-cusps corresponding to isotropic planes containing the isotropic line.
