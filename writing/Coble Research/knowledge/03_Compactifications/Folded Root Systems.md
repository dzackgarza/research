> [!definition] Folded Root Systems
> Let $L$ be a [[Lattice]] containing a [[Root System and k-Roots|root system]] $\Phi$ and $G \subset \operatorname{O}(L)$ a finite group preserving $\Phi$.
> Given a simple root $\alpha_i \in \Phi$, let $[\alpha_i] \subset \Phi$ denote its $G$-orbit.
> 
> The associated **folded root** and **folded root system** are defined by $$ \beta_{[\alpha_i]} \mathrel{\mathop:}= \sum_{\alpha_j \in [\alpha_i]} \alpha_j \in L^G, \quad \Phi^G \mathrel{\mathop:}= \left\{\beta_{[\alpha_i]} \in L^G \mathrel{\Big|} [\alpha_i] \in \Phi/G \right\} $$
> 
> Each folded root corresponds to a folded node in the Dynkin diagram.
> For an involution $I$, $\beta_{[\alpha_i]} = \alpha_i + I(\alpha_i) \in L^G$.
