> [!theorem] Scattone's condition for Eichler transvection orbits [@Sca87] Let $L$ be an even [[Lattice]] that decomposes as $L \xrightarrow{\sim} U^2 \oplus M$ where $M$ is any lattice, and suppose $v, w \in L[k]$ for some fixed $k$.
> Then $$ v \sim_{E(L)} w \Longleftrightarrow [v^*] = [w^*] \in A_L $$ where $E(L)$ is the [[Eichler-Siegel Transformations|Eichler transvection group]].

This theorem is central to distinguishing the orbits of isotropic vectors by studying their projection into the finite discriminant group $A_L$.
