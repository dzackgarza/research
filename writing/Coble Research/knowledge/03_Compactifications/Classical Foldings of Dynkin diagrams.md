> [!remark] Classical Foldings of Dynkin diagrams The process of folding nontrivial diagram automorphisms produces non-simply-laced [[Root System and k-Roots|root systems]] from simply-laced [[Coxeter-Vinberg Diagrams|diagrams]]. The roots in the same orbit under the folding group must be orthogonal.
> 
> Classical examples of foldings of simply-laced Dynkin diagrams include:
> - $A_{2n-1} \to C_n$ via $S_2$ (Horizontal reflection)
> - $D_{n+1} \to B_n$ via $S_2$ (Vertical reflection)
> - $D_4 \to G_2$ via $S_3$ (Rotation by $2\pi/3$)
> - $E_6 \to F_4$ via $S_2$ (Horizontal reflection)
