> [!theorem] Main Theorem: Compactification of $F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}$
> Let $\overline{F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}}$ denote the KSBA compactification of the moduli space $F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}$ of numerically polarized Enriques surfaces of degree $2$. Let $\mathcal{F}_\bullet = \{\mathcal{F}_k\}_{k=1}^5$ be the collection of folded semifans for the $0$-cusps.
> 
> 1. The normalization $\overline{F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}}^\nu$ is isomorphic to the semitoroidal compactification $\overline{F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}}^{\mathcal{F}_\bullet}$ [@AEGS23, Thm. 1].
> 2. This compactification is toroidal over cusps 2, 4, their adjacent 1-cusps, and cusp 35, and strictly semitoroidal over all other cusps.
> 3. The isomorphism is established via an intermediate normalization $B^\nu$ of the Zariski closure of the Noether-Lefschetz locus inside the K3 compactification $\overline{F_{(2,2,0)}}$ [@AEGS23, Sec. 6].
