> [!theorem] Global Extension of the Enriques Involution
> Over the Noether-Lefschetz closure $B \subset \overline{F_{(2,2,0)}}$, there is a universal KSBA family $({\mathcal{X}}_B, \varepsilon {\mathcal{R}}_B)$.
> The fixed-point-free Enriques involution $\iota_{{\mathrm{En}}}$ on the smooth fibers extends uniquely to a global involution on the entire family ${\mathcal{X}}_B$.
> 
> This global involution preserves the slc pair structure, the ramification divisor ${\mathcal{R}}_B$, and commutes with the global del Pezzo involution $\iota_{\operatorname{dP}}$.
> The quotient $({\mathcal{X}}_B, \varepsilon {\mathcal{R}}_B) / \iota_{{\mathrm{En}}}$ forms a flat family of stable Enriques pairs over $B$.
