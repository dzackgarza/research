> [!conjecture] KSBA vs. semitoroidal comparison for polarized Coble moduli After normalization, the KSBA boundary should agree with the semitoroidal compactification defined by restricting the ramification semifan to the polarized Coble locus.

## Proposed statement

- Start with the branchwise polarized Coble locus inside the degree-2 Enriques moduli problem.
- Compactify it:
  - geometrically by KSBA stable pairs,
  - arithmetically by a semitoroidal construction induced from the restricted ramification semifan.
- Compare their normalizations.

## Proof architecture in the inbox

- Restrict the universal K3 stable-pair family over $F(2,2,0)$ to the Coble Noether-Lefschetz locus $D(r^\perp)$.
- Extend the Enriques involution over the stable limits by uniqueness of KSBA limits.
- Descend the ramification divisor.
- Compare the induced boundary stratification with the restricted ramification semifan.
- Prove the [[No-moduli-loss issue in polarized Coble compactification|no-moduli-loss]] statement.
