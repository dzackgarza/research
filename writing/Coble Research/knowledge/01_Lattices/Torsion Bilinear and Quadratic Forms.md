> [!definition] Torsion Bilinear and Quadratic Forms A **torsion bilinear form** is a pair $(G,\beta)$ where $G$ is a finitely generated torsion $\mathbf{Z}$-module and $\beta: G \otimes_{\mathbf{Z}} G \to \mathbf{Q}/\mathbf{Z}$ is a bilinear form.
> 
> A **torsion quadratic form** is a pair $(G,q)$ where $G$ is a torsion module and $q: G \to \mathbf{Q}/\mathbf{Z}$ is a quadratic form.
