> [!theorem] Decomposability of 2-elementary lattices If $L$ is an even [[2-Elementary Lattices|2-elementary lattice]], then $A_{L}$ can be written as a finite direct sum of discriminant forms $p, q, u$, and $v$ subject to the relations:
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> u^2 & = v^2 \\
> p^4 & = q^4 \\
> u \oplus p & = (p \oplus q) \oplus p \\
> u \oplus q & = (p \oplus q) \oplus q \\
> v \oplus p & = q^3 \\
> v \oplus q & = p^3
> \end{aligned}
> $$

Even 2-elementary lattices that admit a primitive embedding into the [[K3 lattice]] $L_{\mathrm{K3}}$ are finite direct sums of basic lattices such as $A_1$, $D_4$, $D_6$, $D_8$, $E_7$, $E_8$, $E_8(2)$, $\langle 2\rangle$, $U$, and $U(2)$.
