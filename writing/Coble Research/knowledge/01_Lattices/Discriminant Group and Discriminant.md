> [!definition] Discriminant Group and Discriminant
> For a nondegenerate [[Lattice]] $(L,\beta)$, the **discriminant group** is the finite abelian group $A_L \mathrel{\mathop:}= L^{\vee} / L$. 
> 
> The order of the discriminant group equals the absolute value of the discriminant, i.e. $|A_L| = |\operatorname{disc}(L)| = [L^{\vee} \mathrel{\Big|} L]$, so $L$ is unimodular if and only if $A_L$ is trivial.
