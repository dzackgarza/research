> [!definition] Isotropic Submodules and Vectors
> For a [[Lattice]] $(L,\beta)$, a submodule $W \subseteq L$ is **isotropic** if $\beta|_W = 0$ (equivalently, $W \subseteq W^{\perp L}$). 
> 
> An **isotropic vector** is an element $v \in L$ with $\beta(v,v) = 0$. 
> 
> The **Witt index** $\operatorname{WI}(L)$ is the maximal rank of an isotropic sublattice.
