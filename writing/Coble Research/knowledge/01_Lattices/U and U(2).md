> [!example] $U$ and $U(2)$
> The hyperbolic [[Lattice]] $U$ is unimodular, so $U^\vee = U$ and its [[Discriminant Group and Discriminant|discriminant group]] $A_U$ is trivial.
> 
> For the [[Scaled Lattices|scaled lattice]] $U(2)$, with Gram matrix $\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$, the [[Dual Lattice|dual]] is $U(2)^\vee = \frac{1}{2}U = U$ and we have $A_{U(2)} = U/U(2) \cong (\mathbf{Z}/2\mathbf{Z})^2 \cong U/2U$.
