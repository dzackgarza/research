> [!proposition] Finiteness of embeddings for even unimodular lattices
> If $S$ and $L$ are even [[Lattice|lattices]] and $L$ is unimodular, then $\operatorname{Emb}(S, L)$ is a finite set.


## Proof Idea
By Nikulin, a primitive embedding $S \hookrightarrow L$ is determined by an isometry $\gamma: A_S \xrightarrow{\sim} A_T(-1)$. Equivalence of embeddings depends on conjugacy under $\operatorname{O}(A_T)$. Since discriminant groups are finite, $\operatorname{Isom}(A_S, A_T)$ and $\operatorname{O}(A_T)$ are finite. Moreover, $\operatorname{Emb}(S, L)$ depends only on the isometry class of $S$, and $\operatorname{cl}(S)$ is finite, yielding the result.
