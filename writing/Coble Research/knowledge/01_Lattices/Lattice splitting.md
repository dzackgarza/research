> [!definition] Lattice splitting Let $S \hookrightarrow L$ be a primitive embedding of [[Lattice|lattices]] with $T \mathrel{\mathop:}= S^{\perp L}$ its primitively embedded orthogonal complement.
> 
> We say that $S$ **splits** $L$ if $L = S \oplus T$, or equivalently, if $L$ is a trivial index 1 [[Overlattices and Gluing Theory|overlattice]] of $S \oplus T$.
