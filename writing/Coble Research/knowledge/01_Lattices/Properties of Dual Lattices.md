> [!remark] Properties of Dual Lattices
> We summarize some standard properties of dual lattices:
> - Duality commutes with direct sums, $(L \oplus M)^{\vee} = L^{\vee} \oplus M^{\vee}$.
> - If $L$ has [[Gram Matrix|Gram matrix]] $G_\beta$ in a basis $B_L$, then the dual basis satisfies $B_{L^{\vee}} = (B_L^t)^{-1}$, and the Gram matrix of the dual form is $G_{\beta^{\vee}} = G_\beta^{-1}$.
> - The discriminant of the dual satifies $\operatorname{disc}(L^{\vee}) = 1/\operatorname{disc}(L)$.
> - The dual of a scaled lattice is $(L(m))^{\vee} = L^{\vee}(1/m)$.
