> [!definition] Involution Decomposition of Lattices
> Let $L$ be an even nondegenerate [[Lattice]] and $G = \langle I \rangle \cong \mathbf{Z}/2\mathbf{Z}$ be generated by an involution $I \in \operatorname{O}(L)$. The [[Invariant and Coinvariant Sublattices|invariant and coinvariant sublattices]] $L^G$ and $L_G$ are primitive and orthogonal.
> 
> Each $v \in L$ decomposes orthogonally, up to a factor of 2, as:
> $$ 2\cdot v = v_+ + v_-, \qquad v_+ = v + I(v) \in L^G, \quad v_- = v - I(v) \in L_G $$
