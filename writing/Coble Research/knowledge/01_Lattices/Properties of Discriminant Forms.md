> [!proposition] Properties of Discriminant Forms
> The [[Discriminant Forms|discriminant forms]] $b_L$ and $q_L$ of a nondegenerate [[Lattice]] $L$ are themselves nondegenerate. 
> 
> For any $[v], [w] \in A_L$, the bilinear and quadratic forms are related by $\beta_{A_L}([v],[w]) = \frac{1}{2}(q_{A_L} ([v]+[w]) - q_{A_L} ([v]) - q_{A_L} ([w]))$, where multiplication by $\frac{1}{2}$ is interpreted as an isomorphism $\mathbf{Q}/2\mathbf{Z} \to \mathbf{Q}/\mathbf{Z}$.
