> [!definition] Primitive and Saturated Sublattices
> A sublattice $S \leq L$ is **primitive** (or **saturated**) if the quotient module $L/S$ is torsion-free. An element $v \in L$ is **primitive** if the sublattice $\langle v \rangle_{\mathbf{Z}}$ is primitive.
