> [!example] $E_8(2)$ lattice The lattice $E_8(2)$ frequently appears in moduli-theoretic applications.
> It is the [[Scaled Lattices|scaled]] version of the [[E_n root lattices|E_8 root lattice]].
> 
> A direct determinant computation shows that $\operatorname{disc}(E_8(2)) = 2^8$, and we can write $$ E_8(2)^{\vee} \xrightarrow{\sim} \frac{1}{2}E_8 \implies A_{E_8(2)} \cong E_8/2E_8 \cong \frac{1}{2}E_8/E_8 \cong \mathbf{Z}_2^8 $$
