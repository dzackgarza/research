> [!definition] Divisibility
> Let $L$ be a [[Lattice]] with form $\beta$, and let $v \in L$.
> The **divisibility** of $v$ in $L$, denoted $\operatorname{div}_L(v)$, is the positive generator of the ideal $\{\beta(v, w)\mathrel{\Big|} w \in L\} \subseteq \mathbf{Z}$.
