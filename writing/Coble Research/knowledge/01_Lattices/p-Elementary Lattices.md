> [!definition] p-Elementary Lattices
> A [[Lattice]] $(L,\beta)$ is **$p$-elementary** if its [[Discriminant Group and Discriminant|discriminant group]] $A_L$ is a $p$-elementary abelian group, i.e., $A_L \cong (\mathbf{Z}/p\mathbf{Z})^a$ for some integer $a \geq 0$.
