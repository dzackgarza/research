> [!example] $F_4$ and $G_2$ root lattices The [[Root lattice|root lattice]] $F_4$ is realized as a root system in $\mathbf{Z}^4$ with $R(F_4)$ consisting of vectors $\pm e_i$ for $1\leq i \leq 4$, $\pm e_i \pm e_j$ for $1\leq i < j \leq 4$, and those of the form $\frac{1}{2}(\sum \pm e_i)$ where the number of minus signs is even.
> A system of simple roots is: $\alpha_1 = e_2 - e_3$, $\alpha_2 = e_3 - e_4$, $\alpha_3 = e_4$, $\alpha_4 = \frac{1}{2} ( e_1 - e_2 - e_3 - e_4 )$.
> 
> The root lattice $G_2$ embeds into $\mathbf{Z}^3_{\Delta}\subseteq \mathbf{Z}^3$ (i.e. those $x\in \mathbf{Z}^3$ with $\sum x_i = 0$). The root system $R(G_2)$ is generated by roots of the form $\pm(e_i - e_j)$ and $\pm(2e_i -e_j-e_k)$, yielding 12 total roots.
> A simple system is: $\alpha_1 = e_1 - e_2$, $\alpha_2 = - e_1 + 2 e_2 - e_3$.
