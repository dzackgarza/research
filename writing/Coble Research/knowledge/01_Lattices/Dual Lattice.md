> [!definition] Dual Lattice
> For an integral [[Lattice]] $(L,\beta)$, the **dual lattice** is the $\mathbf{Z}$-module of linear functionals $L^{\vee} \mathrel{\mathop:}= \operatorname{Hom}_{\mathbf{Z}}(L, \mathbf{Z})$.
