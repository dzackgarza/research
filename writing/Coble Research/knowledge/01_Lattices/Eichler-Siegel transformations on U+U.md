> [!theorem] Eichler-Siegel transformations on $U \oplus U$
> There is an isometry $\phi: U \oplus U \to (\operatorname{Mat}_{2 \times 2}(\mathbf{Z}), 2\cdot \operatorname{det})$. 
> 
> Under $\phi$, the group generated by [[Eichler-Siegel Transformations|Eichler-Siegel transformations]] corresponds to the image of $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z}) \times \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbf{Z})$ acting on a matrix $X$ by $(A, B)\curvearrowright X \mathrel{\mathop:}= A X B^{-1}$.
