> [!theorem] Classification of indefinite unimodular lattices
> Any indefinite unimodular [[Lattice]] is determined up to isometry by its rank, index, and parity. The same is true for _definite_ unimodular lattices $L$ with $\operatorname{rank} L \leq 8$.


> [!theorem] Classification of small unimodular lattices
> Let $L$ be _any_ unimodular integral lattice, definite or indefinite, with $\operatorname{rank}_{\mathbf{Z}} L \leq 4$. Then either:
> - $L$ is odd and $L \xrightarrow{\sim} \mathrm{I}_{p, q}$ for some $p, q$, or
> - $L$ is even and either $L \xrightarrow{\sim} U$ or $U^2$.
