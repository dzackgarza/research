> [!example] $E_n$ root lattices The [[Root lattice|root lattice]] $E_8$ is the unique, even, unimodular, positive-definite lattice of rank 8, and admits an isometry $$ E_8 \xrightarrow{\sim} \{ x \in \mathbf{Z}^8 \cup ( \mathbf{Z}^8 + \frac{1}{2}v_8 ) \mathrel{\Big|} \sum_{i=1}^8 x_i \equiv 0\pmod{2\mathbf{Z}}\} \hookrightarrow \mathbf{Q}^8 $$ where $v_8 = (1,\cdots, 1)$.
> 
> The root lattice $E_7$ can be constructed as $\operatorname{Ann}_{E_8}(L_1)$ for any sublattice $L_1 \leq E_8$ isometric to $A_1$.
> Similarly, $E_6=\operatorname{Ann}_{E_8}(L_2)$ for any sublattice $L_2$ isometric to $A_2$.
> 
> ## Properties of $E_8$

> - It contains exactly $2^4\cdot 3\cdot 5 = 240$ roots: 112 difference vectors $\pm e_i \pm e_j$ and 128 half-sum vectors $\frac{1}{2}(\pm e_1 \cdots \pm e_8)$ with an even number of minus signs.
> - There are 8 simple roots comprising $\Phi(E_8)$, and there is a transitive action $W(E_8)\curvearrowright \Phi(E_8)$.
