> [!remark] Toric Surface W Let $Y = \mathbf{P}^1 \times \mathbf{P}^1$ and $\tau: (x,y) \mapsto (-x,-y)$ be the coordinate involution.
> $\tau$ has 4 fixed points.
> The quotient $W = Y/\tau$ is a singular del Pezzo surface of degree 4, with 4 $A_1$ singularities.
> Both $Y$ and $W$ are toric, sharing the same polytope $Q_{4,4} \mathrel{\mathop:}= [0,4]^2$, but with different lattices: $M_Y = \mathbf{Z}^2$ and $M_W = \mathbf{Z}^2_{\mathrm{ev}} \mathrel{\mathop:}= \{ (i,j) \in \mathbf{Z}^2 \mathrel{\Big|} i+j \in 2\mathbf{Z} \}$.
