> [!definition] Moduli Stack $F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}$
> $F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}$ is the Deligne-Mumford stack parameterizing pairs $(Z, L)$ where $Z$ is a smooth Enriques surface and $L \in \operatorname{Pic}(Z)$ is nef and big with $L^2=2$ and $L \cdot C > 0$ for every $(-2)$-curve.
> 
> Its **KSBA compactification** $\overline{F_{{\mathrm{En}}, 2}}$ parameterizes pairs $(Z, \varepsilon R_Z)$ where:
> - $K_Z \equiv 0$
> - $(Z, \varepsilon R_Z)$ is semi-log canonical (slc)
> - $K_Z + \varepsilon R_Z$ is ample
> - $R_Z$ is numerically equivalent to $2L$ on the smooth locus.
