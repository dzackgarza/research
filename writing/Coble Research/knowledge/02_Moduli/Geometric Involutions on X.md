> [!remark] Geometric Involutions on K3 Double Cover
> For the K3 double cover $X \to Y = \mathbf{P}^1\times\mathbf{P}^1$, there are three distinguished involutions acting on coordinates $(x,y,z)$:
> - $\iota_{\operatorname{dP}}(x,y,z) = (x,y,-z)$ (Deck transformation, K3 quotient, $\iota_{\operatorname{dP}}^*\omega_X = -\omega_X$)
> - $\iota_{\mathrm{En}}(x,y,z) = (-x,-y,-z)$ (Enriques involution, smooth Enriques quotient $Z$, $\iota_{\mathrm{En}}^*\omega_X = -\omega_X$)
> - $\iota_{\mathrm{Nik}}(x,y,z) = (-x,-y,z)$ (Nikulin involution, singular K3 quotient $Z'$ with $\geq 8A_1$ singularities, $\iota_{\mathrm{Nik}}^*\omega_X = +\omega_X$)
> 
> These generate a faithful representation of the Klein 4-group $\mathbf{Z}_2^2 \hookrightarrow \operatorname{Aut}(X)$.
