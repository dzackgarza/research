# Formulae

:::{.remark}

Let $f: Y = \Bl_V X \birational X$ be the blowup of $X$ along a subvariety $V$.
Then one has the adjunction formula

$$
K_Y = f^* K_X + (\codim_V(X) - 1)E
    .
$$

Note that if $X$ is a surface and $V$ is a divisor, this recovers
$$K_Y = f^* K_X + E.$$
:::

:::{.remark}

Let $f: Y\to X$ be a branched cover with branch locus $B$ and ramification locus
$R$.
Then

$$
K_Y = f^* K_X + R, \qquad R \da \sum_{p\in X} \mathrm{len}\qty{ \Omega_{X/Y, p}} [p].
$$
:::
